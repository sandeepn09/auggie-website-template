Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Augie?

    Augie is a revolutionary mobile app based platform enabling you to pay your monthly bills, yet earn a credit rating from it instead of using your own debit card.

  • Can I use it in the United States?

    Augie is in the process of launching in the US and will be live early 2021.

  • What is the Augie Referral Program?

    It’s simple. Use your referral code (you get that in your first sign up email) to refer your friends and family. Every one you refer, earns you $1. You receive the $1 for each referral within sixty (60) days of them activating their Augie account.

  • What does it cost to sign up for and use Augie?

    Zero, zilch, Nada. It really costs you nothing to sign up and use the Augie payment platform.

  • How will I know when I can activate my Augie account?

    Oh, we will email you. Sign up here (make this link to the sign up section).

  • What do I need to activate my Augie account?

    Well, you see, we need to make sure you are a real human and not a dog or a cat. To do that, we need you to have an existing checking or savings account with a US Bank. Any bank will do.

  • Is there a Credit Check?

    Well, we assume you have no credit, so we do not check it. Our job is for you to use us to build your credit.

  • Who is the perfect Augie user?

    Anyone who either has limited or no credit history but is paying any bills monthly.

  • What happens after I give you my email and name?

    We keep that in a very secure database then share emails with you on how you activate your account.
